How To Consolidate Coffee Releases

Why?

--You have multiple coffees from different importers in the same stateside warehouse.

When?

--For instance, you purchased 2 bags from a boutique importer. But to save on freight costs, you want to add 8 more bags for a full pallet.

How?

--In one email, include the importers of the coffees you want to release. Add your order details and mention the shipping details.

Hello,
I would like to release:
2 bags Duromina (120kg total) from [insert importer]
8 bags La Plata (560kg total) from [insert importer]
With [insert importer with higher kg total] handling the shipping please.
